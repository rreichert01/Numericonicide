Controls:

Use WASD to move. 
Use "Q" to switch weapons
Use "R" to reload your weapon
Use Space to shoot
Use Shift to sprint
Use Tab to point gun upwards.


Powerups:

The heart powerup will restore you to full health. 
The shield powerup will block all incoming damage for 10 seconds. 


Goal:

Collect the letters at the end of each level. 
Form the word iceT.
Defeat 8!
Warning, 8 has a gravitational pull and will kill you instantly if it touches you. 
You must defeat 8 before the end of game timer runs out... or he becomes infinity.

**Note** The game's lore is based off of Rick and Morty: Season 7, Episode 8: Rise of the Numbericons: The Movie
